Recorded Journeys

Recorded Journeys is a practice that focuses on individual needs and goals. It is a gentle form of yoga that uses props to support the body in passive stretches. This practice can help reduce stress, anxiety, and depression, while also enhancing mental clarity, emotional regulation, and overall well-being.

Common Questions

Is this class suitable for beginners?

Yes! While Vinyasa is dynamic, our instructors provide modifications for every level. We recommend arriving 10 minutes early to speak with the teacher if it's your first time.

What should I bring?

A yoga mat, comfortable clothing, and water. We provide mats for rent if you don't have one, and all props (blocks, straps) are available in the studio.

How often should I practice?

For best results, we recommend 2-3 sessions per week. However, even one session a week can lead to significant improvements in flexibility and stress levels.
